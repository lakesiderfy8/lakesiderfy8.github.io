The Three Fundamental Principles

At the heart of *The Secret Doctrine* lie three core propositions that form the bedrock of Theosophical thought. They describe the nature of ultimate reality, the cyclical life of the universe, and humanity's divine destiny. Click on each card to reveal its meaning.

The Absolute

An Omnipresent, Eternal, Boundless, and Immutable PRINCIPLE.

This is the ultimate, unknowable reality, a "Be-ness" rather than a being. It is the causeless cause of all, beyond human conception, impersonal and absolute.

Cyclical Eternity

The Eternity of the Universe as a boundless plane of periodic activity.

The universe undergoes endless cycles of manifestation (Manvantaras) and dissolution (Pralayas), like the out-breathing and in-breathing of a "Great Breath."

The Universal Soul

The fundamental identity of all Souls with the Universal Over-Soul.

Every soul is a divine spark on a mandatory pilgrimage of incarnation, governed by Karma and Reincarnation, to eventually realize its own divinity.

Cosmic Evolution: The Root Races

A controversial and complex part of Theosophy is the doctrine of Root Races. It describes humanity's evolution through seven vast epochs on different continents, many now lost to history. This is not a biological or ethnic classification, but a spiritual and physical evolutionary framework. Click on a race to view its description.
